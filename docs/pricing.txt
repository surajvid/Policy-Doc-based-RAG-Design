ABCD AI — Pricing & Plans

Overview
ABCD AI offers a usage-based SaaS pricing model designed for growing sales and marketing teams. 
Customers are billed monthly based on subscription tier plus usage overages where applicable.

------------------------------------------------------------

Starter Plan
Price: $49 per month

Best for: Small teams and early-stage startups.

Includes:
- Up to 5 team members
- 10,000 AI query credits per month
- Document upload (up to 200 documents)
- Basic analytics dashboard
- Email support

Overage:
Additional AI credits are billed at $0.005 per credit.

------------------------------------------------------------

Growth Plan
Price: $149 per month

Best for: Scaling B2B teams.

Includes:
- Up to 20 team members
- 50,000 AI query credits per month
- Unlimited document uploads
- Advanced analytics dashboard
- CRM integration (HubSpot, Salesforce)
- Priority email & chat support

Overage:
Additional AI credits are billed at $0.004 per credit.

------------------------------------------------------------

Enterprise Plan
Price: Custom pricing (contact sales)

Best for: Large organizations with advanced needs.

Includes:
- Unlimited team members
- Unlimited AI query credits (fair usage policy applies)
- Dedicated account manager
- Custom integrations
- On-premise deployment option
- SLA with 99.9% uptime guarantee
- SSO & advanced security features

Overage:
Enterprise plans are negotiated individually. 
Custom usage limits and volume discounts may apply.

------------------------------------------------------------

Free Trial
ABCD AI offers a 14-day free trial for all new customers.
No credit card required.
Trial includes full access to Growth Plan features with a limit of 5,000 AI query credits.

------------------------------------------------------------

Discounts
Annual billing receives a 20% discount compared to monthly billing.
Non-profit organizations may apply for additional discounts.

------------------------------------------------------------

Refund Policy
All paid subscriptions are non-refundable after 7 days of purchase.
Enterprise contracts follow custom refund terms defined in the agreement.

------------------------------------------------------------

Add-ons
- Extra AI credits pack: $100 for 25,000 credits
- Dedicated onboarding support: $1,000 one-time fee
- Custom model fine-tuning: Contact sales

------------------------------------------------------------

Support Levels
Starter: Email support (48-hour response time)
Growth: Priority email & chat (24-hour response time)
Enterprise: Dedicated account manager + 4-hour SLA

------------------------------------------------------------

Security & Compliance
- SOC 2 compliant infrastructure
- Data encryption at rest and in transit
- Role-based access control
- Audit logs available on Enterprise plan
